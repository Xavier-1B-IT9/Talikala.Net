Art Gallery â€” Purpose


Just to live in this world, a lot of women and children have had no other choice but to resort to prostitution. To earn money to create a living for themselves or their family, they are forced to sell their bodies and whole beings to the cruelty of the world and its people. In society, these victims are viewed as immoral and sinful and are treated just like objects that could be played with anytime and thrown away after use. We firmly believe that this is awfully unfair and callous, these victims are humans, they have their own dignities and integrities, feelings, and personalities and qualities that make them who they are, and they too, have the right to live good, and peaceful lives just like all of us. Through this gallery page, we aim to help others empathize or sympathize with these victims through a showcase of different artworks in an attempt to tell the story of their lives/experiences they might have faced. We hope to make society more aware of the state of these women and children to help them with the challenges they face in life, and so that they are viewed and treated as equals just like every other human being.
