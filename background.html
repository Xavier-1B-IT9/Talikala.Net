Institutions Background and Significance


Project Background

One of the biggest problems affecting the community, especially during the start of the pandemic, is the inadequacy of sustenance.

The existing facilities, such as kitchens and resources like food supplies, cannot accommodate the number of benefactors within the targeted area.

Project Significance

Talikala’s issue of lack of food assistance has been greatly prominent in the organization since the pandemic started.

Food assistance is a must-have in institutions since it is what supplies both the beneficiaries and staff with energy and nutrients. Without this factor, they would be greatly at risk of health problems such as stunted growth, eye problems, diabetes and heart disease. Our project will help solve the problem of food scarcity by providing the institution with a nutritious meal — sandwich with eggplant chips.

Through our project, we will be able to solve the institutions problem with food assistance and give both the beneficiaries and benefactors enough energy to carry out the activities that they do in the organization.



Proposed Intervention/Project Proposal


Proposed Product

In order to address the issue of food scarcity at the Talikala institution, we came up with the idea of preparing sandwiches with eggplant chips as a nutritious and healthy meal. The institution's food supply would be increased, more members would be able to eat healthy food, and more money would be generated to support the institution's resources.

Product Benefits

The institution's potential to consume more food, get the nutrients they need for good health, and earn more money to support their resources makes our proposed solution a beneficial one. This is because we are aware that the majority of the residents of the Talikala institution are victims who’s physical and mental wellbeing have not been taken care of prior to being a part of Talikala. However, the majority of our meal, which is made up of vegetables rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, may give women and children an increase in energy and productivity.








Project Doability

Because it is quick, simple, and inexpensive, we believe that this meal is preferable to other alternatives. Speaking for our members that do not have the best skills in cooking and for some individuals in the institution, this meal would still be achievable.

Preperation Needed

The cooking or preparation procedure would be easier to understand if resources were gathered to make this meal available at home or in the institution. Other options, that are more expensive and difficult to implement, would result in more mishaps during the process and, in some cases, would worsen Talikala's problem or situation.


Collaboration

Furthermore, in order to continue and implement this solution or project, we would require the collaboration of our group and the partner institution in order to have an agreeable workplace agreement and a comfortable relationship. Proper teamwork within the group is also essential in delegating tasks and making the cooking process faster and more organized. With these, we could successfully implement and solve Talikala's problem with food scarcity.
